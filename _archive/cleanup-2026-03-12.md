### AviationAlert note
The three definitions were structurally different:
- `aviation-service.ts`: `type: 'SIGMET' | 'AIRMET' | 'CWA' | 'PIREP'`, `severity: 'low' | 'moderate' | 'severe' | 'extreme'`
- `context-manager.ts`: `type: 'SIGMET' | 'AIRMET'`, `severity: string` (narrower — stale)
- `AlertTicker.tsx`: same as aviation-service + `rawText?: string` (unused in component)

Canonical version is aviation-service (broadest, used by data layer). The context-manager's narrower version was stale.
